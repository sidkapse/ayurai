Why Option B?
Using position:fixed;bottom:0 on the bottom card guarantees the Next button is always visible — on 375px iPhones, tall Android devices, and everything in between. The hero content adjusts to the remaining space. This is the pattern used by all major onboarding flows (Duolingo, Spotify, Airbnb).
